    About Us

    City Harvest Electricals was incorporated on 15th day of December the year 2012 with three core business areas which include; power lines surveying and construction and supply of Electrical materials, tools and Equipment

    Our Mission

    To provide quality, cost effective and sustainable engineering solutions in the East African region by 2045

    Our Core Values

    Our Vision

    To be among the Top Five Electrical Engineering service providers in East Africa by 2045.

    Services

    We provide Electrical and Building Construction services which include the followings:

    Cable Works

    These include;Laying, cable jointing, cable terminations, mega testing, Pressure testing, Earth resistance testing and so many other activities involved, amongst too are civil works for Low Voltage, Medium and High Voltage ranges up to 132kV.

    Overhead PowerLines

    Construction of overhead power lines and their maintenance. We also carry out line clearance for overhead lines, Substation designing and construction

    Project Management Services

    Project management services with the latest software of Microsoft Project to assist in resource planning, budgetary purposes, cost control as well as project control and monitoring needs. The project management discipline in our works caters for the Cost, Quality and Time aspects, which affect the project environment hence preventing delays, project mismanagement and financial losses

    Switchgear servicing and maintenance

    Transformers maintenance

    Maintenance and overhaul of diesel Generators

    Refurbishments

    Major refurbishments/rehabilitation of substation’s switchgears/accessories; CTs VTs,Surge arresters ,ACSR Conductors, Line taps, VCBs, TXs, SF6 CB, Air break switches, Gantries,etc

    Switchgears

    Installations of 33KV Substation switchgears for plant capacity expansion

    factory Transformers

    Overhaul & oil filtration of factory Transformers (0.75-4MVA),Mechanical Maintenance of motors (up to 750KW) in factories & Building Construction.
